## **High Priority Enhancements:**

### 1. **Multiple Victory Conditions**
- Alternative objectives: defeat 10 enemy ships, reach level 10, collect all treasures
- Difficulty modes (Easy/Normal/Hard) with different targets
- Achievement system with badges

### 2. **Ship Classes & Special Abilities**
- Different ship types: Sloop (fast), Galleon (tank), Frigate (balanced)
- Special abilities: speed boost, shield, chain shot, broadside volley
- Cooldown-based ability system

### 3. **Dynamic Weather & Day/Night**
- Weather effects: storms slow movement, fog reduces visibility
- Day/night cycle affecting NPC behavior
- Visual atmosphere changes

### 4. **Trading System**
- Port towns on some islands where you can trade jewelry for gold
- Buy cannonballs, repairs, ship upgrades
- Market prices fluctuate

### 5. **Quest System**
- Random bounties on specific NPC ships
- Escort missions protecting merchant ships
- Treasure hunt quests with map clues
